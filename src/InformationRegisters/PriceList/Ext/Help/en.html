The Price list stores daily item prices that are automatically pulled into sales documents. Prices are stored in your company's default currency, which is specified in Accounting settings.

To add a price

  1. In the Sales section, click Price list and then click Create.
  2. Enter or select the following:
    • Period. The start date of the period when the price is in effect.
    • Item. The item name.
    • Price. The item price.
  3. Click Write and close.
