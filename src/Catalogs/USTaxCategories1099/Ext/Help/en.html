The 1099 categories list stores 1099 vendor categories.

The 1099 categories list is only available if the Sales tax (US) check box is selected in Accounting settings. 

To add a category

  1. In the Accounting section, click 1099 categories list, and then click Create. 
  2. Enter or select the following:
    • 1099 category. The category name. 
    • Threshold, $. The 1099 category threshold.
  3. Click Write and close.
