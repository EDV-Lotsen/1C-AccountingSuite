The invoice payment is a document used to record Purchase invoice and Sales return payments.

Invoice payments generate general journal transactions. For detailed information about transactions that are generated when an invoice payment is posted, see the General journal help section.

To enter an invoice payment

  1. In the Bank section, click Invoice payments, and then click Create.
  2. Enter or select the following:
    • Number. By default the system assigns consecutive numbers when documents are saved, and no user input is required.

      You have the option to enter a custom number instead. Document numbers can include prefixes. For example, if you specify the number "IP-1", your next invoice payments are automatically numbered "IP-2", "IP-3", and so on.  
    • Bank account. The account (e.g. bank, cash, credit card, PayPal, etc.) that will receive the funds. 
    • Date. The payment date.

      This field is prefilled with the current date.
    • Company. The company that receives the payment. 
    • Company code. The company code (read-only).

      This field is only available if the Display company codes check box is selected in Accounting settings.

    After you select a company, the system will display all unpaid documents for the company in line items of the Paid invoices list.
  3. Enter the amounts that are being paid against each unpaid document.
  4. In the Memo field, you can enter notes on the invoice payment.
  5. Click Post and close.

    The informational (read-only) section at the bottom of the form contains the following data:
    • Total FCY. The total amount in the transaction currency.

      This field is only available if the Multicurrency check box is selected in Accounting settings. 
    • Total. The total amount in the reporting currency.
