The Lists section stores the lists of companies, items, and other information used in documents. For example, when you create a sales invoice, you select a customer from the list of companies, and items from the list of items.

The Lists section contains the following lists:

  • Companies. Stores the companies you do business with - your customers and vendors.
  • Items. Stores information about items (products or services) that you buy or sell.
  • Countries. Stores the list of countries that you can select when entering addresses.
  • States. Stores states and regions that you can select when entering addresses.
  • Currencies. Stores currencies that you can use for transactions.

    This section is only available if the Multicurrency check box is selected in Accounting settings.
     
  • Exchange rates. Stores currency exchange rates.

    This section is only available if the Multicurrency check box is selected in Accounting settings.
     
  • Payment terms. Stores payment terms. 
  • Units of measure. Stores units of measure.
