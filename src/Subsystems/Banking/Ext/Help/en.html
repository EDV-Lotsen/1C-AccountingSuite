The Banking section automates banking business processes. Automatic document posting creates general journal transactions.

The Banking section contains the following documents:

  • Invoice payment. Records purchase invoice and sales return payments.
  • Cash receipt. Records sales invoice and purchase return payments.
  • Check. Records payments and automates physical check writing.
  • Deposit. Records a bank deposit from a cash receipt or a cash sale to your company's bank account.
  • Bank reconciliation. Streamlines monthly bank closing process. It compares cash receipts and invoice payments recorded in the system with the bank statement.
     

 The Banking section contains the following report:

  • Bank balances. Shows the balances of your company's bank accounts.
